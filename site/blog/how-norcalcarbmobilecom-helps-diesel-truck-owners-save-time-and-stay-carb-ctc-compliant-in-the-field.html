Why Mobile Testing Matters

California is cracking down on emissions violations through programs like HD I/M (Heavy-Duty Inspection and Maintenance). Trucking companies who delay compliance risk fines, registration holds, and downtime.

By choosing a mobile CARB testing provider like NorCal CARB Mobile, you eliminate the delays and get your trucks tested, documented, and back on the road — fast.
