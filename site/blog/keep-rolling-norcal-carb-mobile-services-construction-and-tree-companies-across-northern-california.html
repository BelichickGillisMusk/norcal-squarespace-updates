Published July 16, 2025 · NorCal CARB Mobile

At NorCal CARB Mobile, we understand the challenges faced by construction and tree companies working tirelessly in the field across all Northern California counties. Our mission is to keep your heavy-duty diesel trucks compliant with the Clean Truck Check (HD I/M) requirements without disrupting your workflow. Here’s how we bring our mobile testing services directly to you, ensuring you stay on the road and in business.

On-Site Convenience for Field Operations

Whether you’re clearing land, building infrastructure, or managing tree services in remote areas, downtime can be costly. That’s why our fully equipped mobile units come to your worksite. No need to travel to a testing facility—our certified technicians perform the Clean Truck Check (HD I/M) right where you are, saving you time and resources. Keeping You Compliant with Zero Hassle

Operating diesel trucks over 14,000 lbs in California means adhering to the Clean Truck Check (HD I/M) mandate. Non-compliance can lead to fines or being sidelined. Our team ensures your fleet meets these strict environmental standards with efficient, on-the-spot testing. We handle the paperwork and provide detailed reports, so you can focus on what you do best—building and growing.
