Why Emissions Compliance Matters for Mitchell Concrete

Because their business involves heavy machinery, concrete mixers, trucks, generators, etc., Mitchell Concrete must navigate a web of environmental regulations:

  • CARB (California Air Resources Board) requirements for mobile source emissions — these are not optional.

  • Increased inspections, stricter emissions limits, especially for diesel vehicles and equipment used in construction.

  • Fines, delays, or even shutdowns if equipment fails to meet standards, which = lost time & money.
