What Is the Clean Truck Check?

Administered by the California Air Resources Board (CARB) and enforced by the DMV, the Clean Truck Check (HD I/M) program requires all diesel-powered vehicles over 14,000 lbs GVWR to complete regular SAE J1667 smoke opacity tests and inspections.

Miss a deadline or skip a required test? The DMV may block your registration, potentially sidelining critical vehicles during your busiest season.
