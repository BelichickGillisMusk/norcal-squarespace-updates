What We Test



As part of the California Clean Truck Check (HD I/M program), we provide:


  • ✅ Vehicle Emissions Control Equipment Inspection

  • ✅ SAE J1667 Smoke Opacity Testing

  • ✅ Visual checks of diesel particulate filters (DPFs)

  • ✅ Inspection of Verified Diesel Emission Control Strategy (VDECS) labels

  • ✅ Compliance test reports for DMV and CARB documentation

Who Needs This Test?



If you operate any diesel, hybrid, or alternative fuel truck over 14,000 lbs GVWR in California, you’re required to meet HD I/M testing requirements. This includes:


  • Box trucks

  • Day cabs and sleeper cabs

  • Delivery vans

  • Construction fleet vehicles

  • Tow trucks

  • School and shuttle buses

  • And more
