What Is the CARB Clean Truck Check?

By NorCal CARB Mobile · Updated June 2026 · 4 min read

Meta description: CARB’s Clean Truck Check is California’s emissions program for heavy trucks. Here’s who needs it, OBD vs OVI testing, deadlines, and what it costs.

If you run diesel trucks in California, the Clean Truck Check (CTC) is the emissions test you now have to pass — twice a year for many fleets. Here’s the plain-English version.

Who needs a Clean Truck Check?

Most non-gasoline vehicles over 14,000 lbs GVWR registered or operating in California…

OBD vs OVI — which test do I need?

Newer trucks (2013+) usually take the OBD test ($75). Older or non-OBD vehicles take the OVI smoke/opacity test ($199).
