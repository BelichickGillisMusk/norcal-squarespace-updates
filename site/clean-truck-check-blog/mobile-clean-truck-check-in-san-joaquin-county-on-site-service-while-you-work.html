Are You a Diesel Truck Owner or Fleet Manager in San Joaquin County?

California’s CARB Clean Truck Check (HD I/M Program) is now a mandatory inspection for diesel trucks over 14,000 lbs Gross Vehicle Weight Rating (GVWR). The DMV requires proof of this test every 6 months to stay compliant and legally registered.

If you’re in San Joaquin County, we make compliance fast, easy, and affordable.
