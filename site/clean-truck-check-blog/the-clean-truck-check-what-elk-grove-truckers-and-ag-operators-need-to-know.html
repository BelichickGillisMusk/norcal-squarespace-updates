The Clean Truck Check: What Elk Grove Truckers and Ag Operators Need to Know

Under California’s Clean Truck Check program (formerly HD I/M), nearly all diesel vehicles over 14,000 lbs GVWR must now pass emissions inspections — or risk registration blocks and fines.

  • Logistics and On-Road Trucks must be tested twice per year (semi-annually).

  • Agricultural Vehicles need to be tested once per year, even if they’re only used seasonally or within a limited range.

This includes:

  • Tractor-trailers

  • Reefers (refrigerated trucks)

  • Flatbeds used for produce and vineyard transport

  • Agricultural service vehicles

  • Haulers and pickups supporting field operations
