Introducing Our CARB Compliant -New WAGER 6500+ Smoke Opacity Meter

Published August 8, 2025 · NorCal CARB Mobile

At NorCal CARB Mobile, we’ve always believed in combining cutting-edge technology with exceptional service to keep fleets compliant and productive. That’s why we’re proud to announce the arrival of our new WAGER Model 6500+ Smoke Opacity Meter—a seamless evolution from the dependable system we’ve trusted for years.

A Trusted Legacy – Now Enhanced for the Future

The 6500+ carries forward everything we valued in our previous system, but with a totally refreshed internal design featuring the latest components and technologies. The result? A reliable, accurate, and portable testing solution built to meet today’s strict CARB standards while preparing for the challenges of tomorrow.

Precision in Every Test

The Model 6500+ accurately detects, measures, and displays the opacity of smoke emitted by diesel engines. These readings aren’t just about compliance—they also provide valuable insight into engine combustion efficiency, helping fleet operators identify maintenance needs that can improve fuel economy and performance.

Smart Connectivity & Professional Reporting

The system comes complete with:

  • PC interface cable and software for downloading and archiving test results.

  • The ability to print professional reports on standard 8 ½ x 11 letterhead—featuring your company logo and address—to present to your customers.

  • An ambient correction calculator built into the software, making it easy to adjust readings for higher elevations, ensuring accuracy anywhere we test.

These features not only improve record-keeping but also help build repeat business by giving clients clear, branded documentation of their compliance.

Why It Matters for Our Clients

With the 6500+, we can continue to provide fast, on-site CARB-compliant testing with greater efficiency and accuracy than ever before. Whether we’re at your yard before sunrise or meeting you at a job site, you can trust that our equipment delivers consistent, verifiable results—helping you stay on the road and in compliance.

NorCal CARB Mobile is committed to investing in the best tools and technologies for our clients. The WAGER 6500+ is more than just an upgrade—it’s a promise that we’ll keep delivering top-tier service backed by industry-leading equipment.
