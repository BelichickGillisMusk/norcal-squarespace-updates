Everything You Need to Know About CARB Compliance - Now on One Site

Published December 22, 2025 · NorCal CARB Mobile

We’ve just launched comprehensive service pages covering every aspect of California diesel truck compliance. Whether you’re managing a 50-truck fleet or operating a single semi, these resources will help you stay CARB-compliant and avoid costly penalties.

New Service Pages:

Mobile CARB Testing - Learn about our on-site testing process. We bring professional OBD and smoke opacity testing directly to your location anywhere in Northern California. No more shop visits, no more downtime.

Smoke Opacity (J1667) Testing - PSIP requirements explained. If CARB sent you a smoke testing notification letter, this guide explains exactly what you need to do, when you need to do it, and what happens if you don’t.

DPF Inspection - Protect your investment with proactive diesel particulate filter diagnostics. DPF issues are the #1 cause of CARB test failures and can cost $5,000+ in penalties if equipment has been deleted.

Fleet Compliance Audits - Keep your entire operation CARB-compliant. One non-compliant truck can trigger an audit of your entire fleet. Our proactive audits identify violations before CARB inspectors find them.

Location-Specific Guides:

Sacramento Region - Coverage from Davis to Folsom and beyond. Discover local resources and enforcement timelines.

Bay Area - Understand regional requirements and mobile testing availability in the greater Bay Area.

Central Valley - See how we serve clients across Modesto, Fresno, Bakersfield, and more with on-site mobile testing.

Whether you need one-off smoke testing, a complete fleet audit, or have questions about complying with PSIP and Clean Truck Check, our new pages provide everything you need to stay compliant. Explore now and bookmark us to stay ahead of ever-changing CARB regulations.
