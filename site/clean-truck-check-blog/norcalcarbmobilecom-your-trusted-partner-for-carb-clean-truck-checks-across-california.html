About the Clean Truck Check Program

The CARB Clean Truck Check program ensures heavy-duty vehicles comply with emissions standards to promote cleaner air across California. NorCalCARBMobile.com is your go-to partner for navigating compliance, offering services for vehicle owners, fleet operators, freight contractors, and more.

Our Services

  • Compliance Support: Guidance on semi-annual and annual requirements for agricultural vehicles, motorhomes, and other applicable vehicles.

  • Testing and Reporting: Assistance with emissions testing, On-Board Diagnostics (OBD) devices, and CTC-VIS database reporting.

  • Training and Resources: Access to tester training, fact sheets, and training videos in English, Español, and ਪੰਜਾਬੀ to simplify compliance.

  • Statewide Coverage: From Northern to Southern California, we provide on-site and remote support to meet your needs.

Key Resources

  • Fact Sheets: Covering general requirements, exemptions, DMV registration holds (SB 210), and specific guidelines for ports, railyards, and emergency vehicles.

  • Outreach Materials: Bi-fold overviews in English, Español, and ਪੰਜਾਬੀ to help you understand the program.
