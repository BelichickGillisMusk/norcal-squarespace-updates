✅ What is the Clean Truck Check?

The California Air Resources Board (CARB) launched the Clean Truck Check (formerly HD I/M) to reduce diesel emissions from medium- and heavy-duty vehicles with a GVWR over 14,000 lbs.

This includes:

  • Diesel trucks (commercial and personal use)

  • Motorhomes over 14K lbs

  • Box trucks

  • Day cabs and long-haul sleepers

  • Large work vans and RVs

Compliance requires two things:

  1. Annual emissions testing (either OBD or opacity/visual)

  2. Annual registration in the CTC-VIS portal, with a per-vehicle fee
