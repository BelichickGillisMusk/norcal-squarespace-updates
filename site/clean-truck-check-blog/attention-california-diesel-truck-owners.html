⚙️ What Is the Clean Truck Check?

California’s new HD I/M regulation (aka the Clean Truck Check) requires diesel truck owners — including out-of-state vehicles — to pass periodic emissions compliance testing. This includes:

  • OBD testing for vehicles equipped with On-Board Diagnostics

  • Emissions control equipment verification

  • Digital upload of your results directly to CARB

If you’re not testing, you’re at risk of fines, citations, or losing road access.
